  1. Remove the bones from the <IngredientLink id="chicken" />, and cut the flesh into thumb sized pieces; leaving the skin on
  2. Puncture the <IngredientLink id="chicken" /> multiple times with a sharp knife, to allow the marinade to saturate the meat
  3. Create the marinade by combining <IngredientLink id="soy" />, <IngredientLink id="mirin" />, <IngredientLink id="sake" />, <IngredientLink id="sesameOil" />, <IngredientLink id="ginger" />, and <IngredientLink id="garlic" /> in tall-sided container

In order to get a good crisp on the outside of the karÄge, we'll fry the <IngredientLink id="chicken" /> 3 times. The first time, we'll use a lower temperature - <InlineQuantity id="fryTemp1" /> - for a longer time - <InlineQuantity id="fryTime1" />.
This allows the heat to reach the inside of the meat. The 2^nd^ and 3^rd^ fries drive off more of the moisture (primarily from the outside), and crisp up the <IngredientLink id="cornflour" />.
We limit the duration here, and allow resting time for the internal temperature of the <IngredientLink id="chicken" /> to drop a bit, to avoid overcooking the <IngredientLink id="chicken" /> in the middle.
